clustimage's documentation!
============================

``clustimage`` Python package clustimage is for unsupervised clustering of images.
Clustering of images is performed after carefully following the steps of pre-processing, feature-extracting, feature-embedding and cluster-evaluation.
``clustimage`` also provides functionalities to detect and extract faces. In addition it can also predict new unseen samples to clusters of images with high similarity.
